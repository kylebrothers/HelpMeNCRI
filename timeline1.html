NCRI Timelines - Access to Services

  1. You submit request to Access to Services

    Timeline estimates begin when your protocol is determined to be complete and final by our team.

  2. Regulatory Team submits to IRB and NHRO

    Typical timeline: 2 weeks

  3. IRB reviews and sends approval

    Typical timeline: 4 weeks

  4. NRI Legal Review

    Typical timeline: 3 weeks

  5. NHC HR Review

    Typical timeline: 8 weeks

    Only applies when protocol involves collecting survey or interview responses from NHC employees

  6. NHRO Approval
